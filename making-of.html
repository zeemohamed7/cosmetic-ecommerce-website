Design & Code Justifications

Our Brand Palette

The Celeste color palette was chosen to reflect sophistication, calmness, and inclusivity. It combines soft lavenders, warm neutrals, and subtle contrasts for a modern and elegant feel.

a) Impact of Design and Color Choices

The choice of a soft lavender color palette aligns with Celeste’s brand identity — calm, elegant, and inclusive. The consistent use of white space improves readability and focuses user attention on products. Typography and layout follow minimalistic principles to enhance clarity and modern appeal. The use of consistent button colors and gentle hover effects ensures accessibility while reinforcing brand cohesion across pages.

b) Impact of HTML, CSS, JS, and Bootstrap on Maintainability

The website uses semantic HTML5 structure for clear content hierarchy, making it easy to update and maintain. Bootstrap’s grid system ensures responsive layouts without extensive custom code, reducing technical debt. Modular CSS styling and consistent class naming promote reusability across pages. Lightweight JavaScript enhances interactivity without affecting performance, ensuring a maintainable and scalable front-end codebase.
